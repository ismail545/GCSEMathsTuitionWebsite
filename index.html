My Teaching Methodology

My teaching strategy is focused on encouraging you to think for yourself and on helping nurture your problem solving abilities. I also aim to nurture logical and analytical thinking in students so that they approach each question step by step with clarity in the exam. It is this methodology that will help students get top grades.
